Agreement

By submitting this form, you agree that, if selected for an AMGEN fellowship, you will:

  • Participate in the 10-week program of full-time laboratory research
  • Check the email you applied to the program with regularly
  • Not attend classes, or hold another job or volunteer position during the term of the Program
  • Present your research during a weekly meeting
  • Participate in the seminar on scientific communication skills
  • Submit a research article of your findings as an electronic file in pdf, signed by principal investigator
  • Attend the Amgen Scholars Symposium in California, the third weekend in July
  • Notify us of any published articles on which your name appears
  • Are a U.S. citizen with documented proof of citizenship
  • Live in housing provided by the Columbia University Amgen Scholars Program
  • Adhere to the Amgen Scholars Program/Columbia University Code of Conduct
  • Complete all surveys related to the Amgen Scholars Program, both during and following completion of the program
  • Inform us of any graduate school program you are accepted into
  • Have not previously participated in the Amgen Scholars Program
  • Report any additional fellowships or sources of funding for the summer
  • Discuss lab results during weekly lab meeting at least once during the summer
